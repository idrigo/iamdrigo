About

From research vessels to enterprise weather

I started in science — oceanography at Moscow State University, graduating with honours, with field seasons as a hydrometeorology engineer on marine expeditions. A Master's in applied mathematics and machine learning at ITMO University pivoted me toward AI-driven forecasting, including Arctic sea-ice prediction.

From academia I moved into industry: leading meteorology research at Windy.app, then four years at Yandex Weather as a Senior Research Meteorologist, where I led 50+ B2B projects across insurance, agriculture, and energy — from climate risk modelling to AI-powered weather products. Today I help OpenWeather's enterprise clients get the most out of weather data.

I remain active in research: most recently, a two-and-a-half-month research cruise in the Tropical Atlantic, studying deep ocean channel currents as a physical oceanographer, with publications currently under review.
